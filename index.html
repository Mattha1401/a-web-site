VakıfBank S.K. VakıfBank Spor Kulübü

VakıfBank Sports Club (Turkish: VakıfBank Spor Kulübü) is a Turkish volleyball club based in Istanbul, Turkey. Founded in 1986, VakıfBank SK are currently one of the best women's volleyball teams in the world, having won the FIVB Volleyball Women's Club World Championship a record four times and the European Champions League five times to date..
